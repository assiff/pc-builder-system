  • Automatically check compatibility between parts.

  • See real-time price updates and performance comparisons.

  • Save and share your builds with others.

  • Buy directly or export your part list.

Whether you want to build a high-end gaming rig, a budget-friendly workstation, or a balanced all-rounder for daily use, PC Builder gives you full control and flexibility.
